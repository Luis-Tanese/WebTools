Format Tokens

DD: Day of month (09)
DDD: Short day (Mon)
DDDD: Full day (Monday)
Do: Ordinal day (9th)
MM: Month (12)
MMM: Short month (Dec)
MMMM: Full month (December)
YYYY: Full year (2024)
WW: Week number (50)
hh: 12-hour (01)
HH: 24-hour (13)
mm: Minutes (25)
ss: Seconds (51)
ms: Milliseconds (131)
tt: AM/PM (AM)
ISO: ISO format
R: Relative time
H: Holiday name
Z: Time zone offset
TZ: Time zone name
